My EGN experience

My experience with EGN has been very interesting. I felt like a lot of the work I did was busy work. Although said I did not take the work as a joke. I understood why the class is important and its mainly to help us get into the mindset of what a software engineer should be thinking about and how he should go about solving solutions to problems. I learned that when programming there are ethics to take into account and when creating technology I want to be the person to produce something that will bring good and not destruction. I want to create something that will help humans and do what I can to make sci-fi reality but safely and with the correct procedures. We also payed a lot of attention to topics like global warming and things that affects everyone on the globe. These were things I never thought about and when we talked about it in class I enjoyed learning about it because in reality I don't think I would have done research like this otherwise. I had a great time with the course as well because I like thinking about all the possibility's. If I could go back and retake this class I would do so only to research and focus on other topics. Although nothing is preventing me from doing my own research.
