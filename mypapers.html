  • a poster of a future solution for facial recognition with bank transactions.

    In this assignment we made groups with a few peers with similar interests, Mine was cyber security. We had theorized about a product that would make bank transactions safer. we saw an opportunity to appeal to bankers and investors who would like more security. We thought first off that banks need to be secure to have any business and investors alike need a place to secure all of their cash. So why don't we make it secure from people who might be monitoring your computer at home like a keylogger.
    To do this we thought we could do facial recognition and throw on 2FA to make it extra secure. This would make your account super personalized because no transaction will be made unless you are on the camera. This would be a good idea in today's society considering a lot of people already have cameras at home on computers for remote work.
    I personally think the project was fun to do and a nice break from coding to just think about something futuristic and working with my group mate was enjoyable as well.
